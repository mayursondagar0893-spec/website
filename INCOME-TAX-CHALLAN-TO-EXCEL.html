Tool

Income Tax / TDS / TCS Challan PDF to Excel

Upload ITNS 280 / 280N / 281 / 282 / 283 challan PDFs — extracted 100% locally, no server upload. Supports FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27) & FY 2026-27 (Tax Year 2026-27).

💡 How to Use this Tool ▾
1
Upload Challans
Click Select Folder to pick an entire folder of PDFs at once, or Select Files to choose individual challans. You can also drag & drop PDFs or folders directly onto the upload area. Sub-folders are scanned automatically.
2
Parse Challans
Click ⚡ Parse Challans (or press Ctrl+Enter). The tool reads each PDF locally in your browser — no data is sent to any server. ITNS 280 / 280N / 281 / 282 / 283 formats are all supported, including the new Tax Year format from AY 2026-27.
3
Filter & Search
Use the filter bar to narrow by ITNS type, Tax Year / AY, PAN, TAN, or Status. The search box matches PAN, TAN, Name, CIN, Challan No., Bank Ref, and more. Click any stat card (Total / Parsed OK / Needs Review) to quick-filter. Total Amount updates live to match your filter.
4
Export to Excel
Click 📊 Export to Excel (or press Ctrl+E) to download a .xlsx file with 3 sheets: Challans (all extracted fields), Code Breakup (section-wise TDS for ITNS 281), and Summary (totals). Only currently filtered records are exported.
5
ITNS Types at a Glance
280 Income Tax (Self-Assessment, Advance Tax)   280N Income Tax – New Challan   281 TDS / TCS   282 Securities Transaction Tax   283 Fringe Benefits Tax
Status "⚠ Check" means PAN/TAN or CIN could not be extracted — open the original PDF to verify.
🔒
100% Private — No Server Upload
All PDF parsing happens entirely inside your browser. Your challan data never leaves your device. Safe for confidential client data.
Keyboard Shortcuts: Ctrl+Enter Parse  ·  Ctrl+E Export to Excel
