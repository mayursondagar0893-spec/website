👋 Who I Am

I am CA Mayur J Sondagar, a practising Chartered Accountant based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. I completed my CA qualification and have been in active practice for over 8 years, advising individuals, small businesses, and corporates on Income Tax, GST, and financial compliance matters.

Beyond my professional practice, I run Taxation Updates — one of India's most trusted personal finance and tax education platforms on social media. What started as a passion project to share clear, actionable tax insights has grown into a community of over 120,000 followers across Twitter, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp who rely on my daily posts, charts, and simplified explainers.

My core belief is simple: every taxpayer deserves to understand their own tax obligations. Tax law in India is dense, frequently amended, and often confusing. I bridge that gap — converting complex CBDT circulars, Budget provisions, and Finance Act amendments into easy-to-read tables, charts, and plain-language summaries that professionals and laypeople alike can use immediately.

💼 What I Do

  • Income Tax Advisory: Tax planning for individuals, HUFs, partnership firms, and private limited companies under the new and old tax regimes.
  • GST Compliance: GST registration, monthly/quarterly return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), reconciliation, and audit support.
  • TDS / TCS Management: Deductee compliance, TDS return filing, lower deduction certificate applications, and challan management.
  • ITR Filing: Accurate and timely filing of Income Tax Returns for salaried employees, business owners, freelancers, and NRIs.
  • Tax Education Content: Daily posts, charts, and reference tables covering Budget updates, new tax law changes, and compliance deadlines.
  • Brand Collaborations: Partnering with BFSI, fintech, and tax-software brands to reach a highly engaged, finance-aware audience of 120K+ followers.

💡 Why I Do This

India has over 8 crore Income Tax return filers and millions of businesses navigating GST compliance — yet formal tax education remains inaccessible to most people. Most taxpayers either over-pay due to lack of awareness, or face penalties because of missed deadlines and incorrect filings.

I started Taxation Updates because I saw this gap every day in my practice. My clients — whether a salaried professional in Pune or a small trader in Ahmedabad — often had the same basic questions that no one was answering in plain language. Every chart I post, every thread I write, and every table I publish on this website is aimed at closing that information gap.

My goal is not just to update you on tax law changes, but to empower you to understand them. An informed taxpayer makes better financial decisions, avoids compliance mistakes, and contributes more confidently to their own financial well-being.
