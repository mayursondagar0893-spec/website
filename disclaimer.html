📅 3. Changing Tax Laws

India's tax legislation — including the Income Tax Act, GST law, TDS/TCS provisions, and related rules — is amended regularly through Finance Acts, CBDT circulars, GST Council notifications, and judicial pronouncements.

Content on this website reflects the law as understood at the time of writing. The following should always be verified from primary sources before reliance:

  • TDS/TCS rates and threshold limits
  • Income tax slab rates and rebates
  • Deadlines for ITR filing, TDS returns, and GST returns
  • Section numbers under the new Income Tax Act 2025 vs Income Tax Act 1961
  • GST exemptions, rates, and HSN code classifications

Always refer to the official Income Tax Department website and GST Portal for the most current information.
