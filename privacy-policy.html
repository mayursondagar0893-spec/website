Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

👋 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how taxationupdates.com ("the Website"), operated by CA Mayur J Sondagar, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit.

This is a static informational website. We do not operate a backend server, run a database, or process payments. Your privacy is important to us.

📊 Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the website. This service collects anonymised data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site)

This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. Google's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

The Website uses the following storage mechanisms:

  • Google Analytics cookies — used by GA4 for session tracking (see above)
  • Theme preference — your light/dark mode choice is saved in localStorage so it persists across visits
  • Bookmarks — in the CA Prompt Library tool, bookmarked prompts are saved to localStorage on your device only

No personal data is stored in cookies or local storage. Local storage data never leaves your browser.

📬 Contact Form

The contact form on the homepage does not send data to any server. When you click "Send Message", the Website opens WhatsApp with your message pre-filled. The message is transmitted directly between you and CA Mayur Sondagar via WhatsApp's platform, subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

We do not store or log any information you enter into the contact form.

🔗 Third-Party Links

The Website may contain links to external sites (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Google Drive, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

🛡️ Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data at risk from a server breach. All tool functionality (challan parsing, prompt library) runs entirely in your browser.

👶 Children's Privacy

This Website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Website after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

📩 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach CA Mayur Sondagar via:

  • WhatsApp: available on the Contact section of the homepage
  • Twitter/X: @TaxationUpdates
  • LinkedIn: Taxation Updates
